Minkwan Kim

I am a Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the Seoul National University under the supervision of Prof. Young Min Kim.

Previously, I worked on 3D displays and computational imaging, focusing on developing novel optical systems and algorithms under the supervision of Prof. Byoungho Lee.

My current research interests lie in 3D reconstruction and 3D scene understanding, focusing on capturing and modeling real-world scenes from visual data. I am particularly interested in developing representations and algorithms that enable accurate, robust, and scalable understanding of complex 3D environments.
